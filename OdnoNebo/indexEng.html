A FERRIS WHEEL
«THE ONE SKY»

THE ONE SKY IS:

The best sight over the city

You `ll enjoy all the best views of Rostov-on-Don 25 km far away

The third Wheel in height in Russia

The height of the attraction is 65 m above the ground-level

Available 365 days in a year

The attraction works the whole year including days off and holidays

The best family attraction

On the version of the only entertainment industry competition in Russia – «The Crystal Wheel 2016»

Safety attraction

The Wheel meets the demands of all state and world standards of quality

Acts of charity

Due to cooperation with the charity organizations more than 4000 were taken for a drive free of charge

Hover the mouse over the active elements to find out more facts about the attraction

High capacity

Our Wheel contains 30 observation cubicles where up to 180 persons can be driven at a time.

Noticeable attraction

The Wheel is situated 130 m above the level of the river Don, the attraction can be seen from the distance of more than 20 km.

Comfort for everybody

The attraction has a separate entrance and a special elevator for hardly mobile groups of people.

Comfort care

All cubicles are equipped with air conditioners and the systems of heating that`s why in summer it is not hot and in winter it is not cold there. In the cubicles of superior comfort there are leather couches and also there is the possibility of choosing music for trip.

Perfect safety

All 8 engines of the Wheel have slipping protection in bad weather and the factor of safety of all constructions is more than 300 percent.

Evening show

In evening time there is beautiful illumination consisting of 22 000 programmable light-emitting diodes.

It`s interesting to everybody

The attraction was built for 9 months and for the first month of working more than 120 000 people attended it.

Comfortable location

The Wheel is situated in the historic center of the city in the park of «October Revolution» next to the theater of M. Gorky.

Well-groomed territory

Around the attraction we laid out 2000 m of tiles and planted 1600 m of qualitative lawn.

Terms of use

  • ○

    The attraction is appropriated for entertainment of children above 6 years old and adults.

  • ○

    The quantity of passengers in a cubicle should be not more than 6 persons.

  • ○

    Children up to 6 years old are allowed for the attraction free of charge, children up to 12 years old should be accompanied by adults (with payment for an adult and a child).

  • ○

    Visitors suffering from disarrangement of vestibular apparatus, mental or nervous disorder are not recommended to attend the attraction.

  • ○

    Visitors with alcohol or drug intoxication are prohibited to attend the attraction.
